About Us

We are the group of three people who has done this news aggregator and analyzer. The news aggregator and analyzer was started in feb 2017 . This site has news from 2 news publisher , times of india and the hindu . It has news articles from 1st january 2016 to 31 december 2016. The idea behind doing this was that It will allow users to do Full-Text Search through various news articles aggregated from the times of india and the hindu . The users who are interested in searching the news articles need not have to go to 2 websites they can view news on single platform and compare the news and they can also further view news from the particular locations and category.
